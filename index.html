About Pine Hill Enclave

Nestled at over 8,000 feet in the Galiyat region of Pakistan, Pine Hill Enclave is a private and elevated residential enclave designed for those seeking privacy, natural beauty, and long-term value. Developed by Marshall Business Management, the project is not a mass-market housing scheme — it is a carefully planned release of a limited number of 1-kanal plots, directly offered by the landowner to a handpicked group of individuals who share the vision of an exclusive, nature-immersed lifestyle.

The development 80KM (2 hours drive) from Zero Point Islamabad, lies along the Murree–Abbottabad Road with year-round road access, stunning pine forest surroundings, and panoramic mountain views. Our mission is to build a gated community where infrastructure comes first: roads, drainage, utilities, high-speed internet, and sustainable water solutions.

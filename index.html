About Pine Hill Enclave

Pine Hill Enclave is a private, high-altitude residential community in Kuza Gali, Galiyat, Pakistan. Designed for discerning buyers seeking privacy, natural beauty, and secure investment, our enclave offers a limited number of 1 kanal plots with direct landowner sales. Enjoy panoramic mountain and forest views, modern infrastructure, and a lifestyle immersed in nature.

Located just 80KM (2 hours) from Islamabad on the Murree–Abbottabad Road, Pine Hill Enclave provides year-round access, robust infrastructure, and a commitment to sustainable development. Our mission is to deliver a secure, gated community where every detail—from roads to utilities to high-speed internet—supports long-term value and comfort.
